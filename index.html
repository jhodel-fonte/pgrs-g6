About Our System

Transparency • Efficiency • Public Service

The Unity: Padre Garcia Service Report System (PGSRS) is a digital platform that helps residents of Padre Garcia report issues, incidents, and concerns directly to their local government.

Through this system, users can:

  • Submit reports easily using a user-friendly interface
  • Track the status of their reports in real time
  • Receive SMS updates from response teams
  • Help build a safer and more responsive community
